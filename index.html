Welcome to PVC: Research, Innovation, and Development (RID)

Find out more about us

The Centre for Innovation Development is established under the Office of Pro-Vice Chancellor for Research Innovation and Development as a vehicle to drive Innovation development, stimulate start up through taking advantage of the value chain under product development. In order to realize the above, CID positions itself as a catalyst that brings together various stakeholders through furthering cooperation in innovation and technology development and its application within the innovation value chain of UNAM.
